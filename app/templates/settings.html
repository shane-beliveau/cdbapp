About This App

Thank you for downloading the Daily News app for Modern Healthcare.

Modern Healthcare's mission is to make our news available wherever our readers need it. Continuing that mission, Modern Healthcare launched this Daily News app for tablets that provides a tablet-optimized view of the healthcare business news executives need most.

The app features the latest healthcare business Top Stories, Breaking News and what's Most Popular with our readers. On the home screen, readers can easily swipe across the headlines for a quick view of what's happening in healthcare right now. Tap a story preview and the app downloads the full story. When you're done reading an article, you can swipe to the other stories in that section or go back to the previews.

For a deeper dive into the topics that interest you most, you can click “My Topics” and choose to read the latest stories from nine topics -- healthcare reform, finance, government, healthcare IT, payers, physicians, safety/quality, supply chain and magazine (stories from our weekly print product).

If you want to save a story for reading later, tap “Add to Briefcase” and the story is saved in your app to read when you have more time. Just tap “My Briefcase” to read all the stories you saved.

We've also assembled the Twitter handles of our staff, under “MH Tweets,” allowing you to follow the 140-character conversations of our editors, reporters and staff, staying on top of the trends and news as they emerge on social networks.

The new Modern Healthcare Daily News app officially launched in beta on April 1, 2013 and officially on May 6, 2013 for all tablets. Following our tablet launch, you'll soon be able to find the app in iTunes and get a version of the app optimized for smartphones as well.

The tablet app is free to download and, for 90 days from April 1, 2013, the news is free to read.

Modern Healthcare is everywhere our readers need us to be. Now we're bringing our award-winning news to you in a tablet-friendly way.

About Modern Healthcare
